Historical Eras

Indus Valley Civilization

One of the world's oldest civilizations, flourishing around 2500 BCE in Mohenjo-Daro and Harappa.

Muslim Rule

Beginning with Muhammad Bin Qasim and later strengthened by the Delhi Sultanate and Mughal Empire.

Pakistan Movement

A political movement led by Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah for a separate homeland for Muslims.

Important Leaders

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Founder of Pakistan and its first Governor-General.

Allama Muhammad Iqbal

National Poet and visionary who inspired the idea of Pakistan.

Liaquat Ali Khan

Pakistan's first Prime Minister and a key leader of the movement.

Timeline of Pakistan

1940

1947

New Constitution adopted.

Today

Pakistan continues to grow as an important nation in South Asia.
